Liam Tully & Johnny Mei

Website for singer-songwriter duo


The low-down: I created a website for a singer-songwriter duo.

One of my good friends, Liam Tully, needed a website to kick off his musical career in LA. We worked together in the past on a previous project, a student feature film, Commencement. We both have personal connections to music and I wanted to make sure that that it was expressed coherently.

At first, I generated some standard mockups based off some sketches Liam had sent me. We weren’t enjoying the results then; it was an exploratory phase.

Then the conversation veered towards Liam’s musical inspirations. He sent me promo photos of his favorite musicians paired with stark, simplistic backgrounds. This prompted me to start going through my vinyl collection. I took notes on visual concepts used in these classic 70s-80s records, the same period that Liam found inspiring. Examples: thin typography, vertical lists, an austere background element, and full width photography.

From there, the look of the site unfolded pretty quickly. We found a great font that matched the aesthetic of the vinyl record covers. We used a wall on Liam’s EP as the background, to give it that gritty and simplistic feel found in the original inspiration photos. I implemented a “news feed” for his social media channels, a page for music, and forms for commissioning songs and booking shows.
