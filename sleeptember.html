Sleeptember

Website for the American Sleep Apnea Foundation


The low-down: I redesigned the home page for the American Sleep Apnea Foundation, improving the user experience and parsability.

I came in to the project with an existing site, albeit one that felt very crowded and overstuffed from a UX perspective.

While there were a lot of great elements to the original home page, we needed to slim it down and create a unified storytelling experience. ASAF’s main goal was to get people to donate and learn about the cause. We decided on three main sections that would best accomplish this: Donate, Participate, and Discuss.

We decided on a orange and blue color scheme and took it from there. As far as imagery went, we didn’t want to stick to sleep-related images, rather, images that conveyed the benefits of a good amount of sleep.
